The People's Web

The decentralzied web is an all-new web where end-users are in control of their own data, the community polices the websites, apps and content distributed on top of it, hackers are rendered useless and where users and developers of the Internet can finally have their freedom back - by design.

Experience The Open Web.

dBrowser allows users and developers to browse the dWeb and develop decentralized websites and applications as well. dBrowser is available for MacOS, Windows and Linux, where you can experience the decentralized and open web for yourself.

One Identity. A World Of Possibilities.

The dWeb and its apps use a universal and decentralized authentication system. One account allows you to use any dWeb-powered apps, it is cryptographically secure and impossible to hack.
