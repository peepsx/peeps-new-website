dWeb Freedom Policy

The dWeb Freedom Policy is a part of dWeb's S.A.I.L. (Shielding American Internet Liberties) Legal Framework and we have adopted its "America First" policies described below to preserve your online freedom.

< Back To Legal
  • Posted: January 23rd, 2021
  • Updated: January 23rd, 2021

I. We Don't Censor & Your Data Is Literally Your Data

Our platforms and data are hosted on the dWeb, which means that it is not possible for us to censor your speech or expression in any form or fashion. This is due to how the dWeb handles your data. You are always in control of your data, regardless of the application you're using, and that goes for all of our platforms. We are anti-censorship and our platforms are entirely censorship-resistant. Even if we have a change in our views on censorship, the dWeb simply won't allow us to censor our users.

II. Open Source

The code for all of our platforms is completely open source and can be found on our GitHub page here. This means our platforms can be publicly audited for backdoors and other malicious backends solutions, none of which exist on any of our platforms.

III. America First

There goes our Twitter page - just kidding. We don't use big tech products like Twitter because of our America First policies, and we will continue to support not only the "Make America" movement, but policies that support Americans everywhere.

IV. Anti-Cancel Culture

Since users are in control of their dWeb destiny, we're just developers (spectators) on the sidelines. We couldn't cancel you or anyone else on our platforms, even if we wanted to. You don't have to worry about cancel culture around these parts.

V. One Web Under God

The dWeb is all about your First Amendment Rights and your right to express your religious views. While most on the centralized web may see you as a radical, God-fearing American, we and all other dWeb developers see you as family. Welcome home.

VI. Family Values & The Preservation Of History

Since we have adopted the dWeb Freedom Policy, we promise to promote Godly family values and do everything in our power to help promote and preserve American History across our platforms. Including our efforts to promote and preserve the work of "The 1776 Commission."

VII. Anti-Terrorism

We do not support domestic or foreign terrorism or terrorist organizations of any kind. We pledge to report all terrorist activity on any of our platforms to law enforcement, because as patriots and conservatives alike, we support law and order.

VIII. We're Making The Web Great Again

We're all about MAGA and MWGA (Make The Web Great Again). This means we're here to support the migration of other organizations to the dWeb and the development of other big tech alternatives. Simply put, we're all about building First Amendment Infrastructure for God-loving Americans like you, and helping others do the same.

"My bias was always to build decentralization into the net. That way it would be hard for one group to gain control. I didn't trust large central organizations. It was just in my nature to distrust them."
- Bob Taylor
Visionary of ARPANET & Godfather Of The Internet
